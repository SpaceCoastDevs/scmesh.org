The Florida Mesh Discord group is coordinating a statewide test of the Meshtastic infrastructure every Monday from 8:00pm-10:00pm Eastern

<LinkButton
  href="/fl-mesh-discord"
  variant="primary"
  icon="discord"
  iconPlacement="start"
>
  Join the the Florida Mesh Discord group
</LinkButton>

To participate please format your messages like this:

> `<your node name>` in `<city>`, Brevard County with `<number of hops in settings>` hops, #weekly-mesh-net message `<message number>`

So it would look something like this:

> scmesh.org in West Melbourne, Brevard County with 7 hops, #weekly-mesh-net message 1

<Aside type="tip">
Increment the `<message number>` with each sent message.
</Aside>

The goal of this net is test the infrastructure and range of the messages, as well as increase engagement on the local mesh by having as many people online and monitoring at the same time. Please report the messages received and any acknowledgements to the <a href="https://discord.com/channels/1235735441592942602/1365372356209414195">#weekly-mesh-net</a> channel.
